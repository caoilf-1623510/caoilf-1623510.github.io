About Us

Our company was founded by Lin Marvin and one of her closest friends, Shay Bush in 1990. Cavities were a huge issue back then because of the amount of sugar that were in candies back then. They wanted to change that. With some experientation, they made all sorts of candies from lollipops, to chocolate, to even gum with catch. They were able to maintain the sweet taste of the candies with minimal addition of sugar. It became a huge hit and it flourished. The company wouldn't be here today if it wasn't for them.

Candy Galore

We strive to ensure that our candy does not use large amounts of sugar. We don't want to be the cause of cavities!
